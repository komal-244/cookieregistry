About the project

What is CookieRegistry?

CookieRegistry is an open reference database for web cookies — inspired by Cookiepedia. It documents cookies used across major platforms, covering their purpose, duration, risk level, and regulatory applicability.

Each cookie entry includes its category (Advertising, Analytics, Marketing, Security, Functionality), plain-language description, expiry details, data collected, and GDPR/CCPA/DPDPA compliance status.

Cookie Categories

Advertising — Ad targeting, impressions, and campaign tracking.


Analytics — Usage patterns, performance, and conversion measurement.


Marketing — User identification and cross-site conversion tracking.


Security — Authentication, session management, and fraud prevention.


Functionality — User preferences and browser capability testing.
